The Power Station Breakeven Costs chart shows the relative breakeven costs at coal and gas stations in the market, factoring in the cost of carbon.

Included Data

This chart using daily closing prices from trades transacted on the Spectron exchange. This means the values are linked to real trading activity.

The coal prices are API2 (delivered into Amsterdam, Rotterdam and Antwerp region in the Netherlands).

The higher the value the higher the cost of generating (including carbon costs), implying a reduced ability to run profitably. Where gas is lower than coal, gas is in merit, when coal is lower than gas, coal is in merit.
